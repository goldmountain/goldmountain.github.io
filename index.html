What I do

Since I'm now working for an agency, it's usual for me to get involved in front-end development, when my main duty is to style up a website using SASS. But I started my design career as identity designer and illustrator, so this is still what I'm mainly focusing on.

I had my identity works featured on various design articles, websites like Logopond and Logomoose, and several international books such as "Logopond Book", "LogoLounge vol. 7", "Logo Nest 01" and "Logo Talks".

You can find my works on several design community websites like Logopond.com, Behance.net and Dribbble.com. But right now, when my work also involves some iconography work, animations, illustrations and identity, I share it all on Dribbble, which is a great place to feature more than one skill.
